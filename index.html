About

I create narrative-driven work spanning across mediums, focusing on cultural diffusion, digital ephemera, and the fragmented formation of adolescent identity. Visually, I am drawn to the tension between organic and geometric forms, metaphorical objects as a storytelling element, and colorplay. Oil paint is my primary medium of choice, due to its alla-prima qualities, which suit the layering of spontaneity and intricacy.
